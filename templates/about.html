Undergraduates participate as student researchers advised by the computer science faculty mentors, which are Rushit Dave (Chair), Jim Seliya, Mounika Vanamala, and Benjamin Fine. Each of the four faculty mentors brings their respective unique experience to the AIMS Research Group, providing a wide spectrum of research opportunities for undergraduates in the computing fields of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and security.
